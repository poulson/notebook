What am I up to?

For the beginning of the Spring quarter, Jack's time is largely split between finalizing the release of version 0.86 of Elemental, teaching Mathematics in the Real World, and slowly starting up a research group. He is currently excited about Elemental finally supporting distributed sparse-direct Interior Point Methods for quadratic programs, distributed sparse-direct generalized least squares problems, and distributed sparse-direct nonsymmetric linear solves (and he now has a newfound appreciation for equilibration and quad-precision arithmetic).
