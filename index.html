What am I up to?

Jack is currently teaching two sections of Linear Algebra with Differential Equations], and his research is currently focused on several aspects of the development of Elemental, such as unifying the various distributed dense data structures in preparation for the introduction of two-dimensional plus low-rank sparse matrix distributions to lift the barrier to scalability inn the library's recently introduced distributed sparse Interior Point Methods (for Linear, Quadratic, and Second-Order Cone Programs). He is also working with several teams on exposing higher-level interfaces and functionality (e.g., through Elemental.jl, CVXPY, SmallK, Skylark, and PETSc).
